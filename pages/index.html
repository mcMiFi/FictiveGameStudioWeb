Our Story

From Magazines to
Game Jams to Publishing

Wafle Gamedev started as a hobby magazine project, fueled by a passion for absurd humor and unconventional storytelling. What began as written satire quickly evolved into interactive experiences through game jams.

After dozens of jam entries that pushed creative boundaries, we transitioned into indie publishing — bringing the same raw, unfiltered energy to full releases.

"We make the games that make you question everything — including why you're playing them."

Magazine Phase

Started with satirical game journalism and creative writing, building a community around dark humor.

Game Jam Era

Dozens of rapid prototypes and experimental titles created under pressure, honing a unique creative voice.

Indie Publishing

Now releasing full titles and supporting other creators who share our vision of bold, satirical gaming.
